# MultiInstrument Strategy Subagent

## Overview
The MultiInstrument Strategy Subagent specializes in cross-asset strategies, portfolio optimization, and multi-instrument trading approaches. It develops sophisticated strategies that span multiple asset classes, exploiting correlations, diversification, and arbitrage opportunities across different markets.

## Core Capabilities

### Cross-Asset Strategy Development
- **Asset Class Integration**: Strategies combining equities, bonds, commodities, currencies, and crypto
- **Correlation Analysis**: Dynamic correlation modeling and correlation-based strategies
- **Arbitrage Opportunities**: Statistical arbitrage and cross-market arbitrage strategies
- **Diversification Optimization**: Portfolio construction across multiple instruments

### Multi-Instrument Trading
- **Pairs Trading**: Statistical arbitrage between related instruments
- **Basket Trading**: Sector, index, and custom basket strategies
- **Hedge Strategies**: Cross-asset hedging and risk management approaches
- **Index Replication**: ETF and index arbitrage strategies

### Portfolio Optimization
- **Risk Parity**: Equal risk contribution across asset classes
- **Mean-Variance Optimization**: Modern portfolio theory implementation
- **Black-Litterman Model**: Incorporating views into portfolio optimization
- **Factor-Based Investing**: Multi-factor portfolio construction

### Dynamic Asset Allocation
- **Tactical Asset Allocation**: Market regime-based asset class rotation
- **Strategic Rebalancing**: Rules-based portfolio rebalancing strategies
- **Risk Management**: Dynamic risk budgeting and position sizing
- **Performance Attribution**: Multi-asset performance decomposition
