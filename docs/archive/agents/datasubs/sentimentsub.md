# Sentiment Subagent

## Overview
The Sentiment Subagent specializes in comprehensive sentiment analysis across news, social media, and market data sources. It processes qualitative information to quantify market sentiment, identify behavioral patterns, and generate sentiment-driven trading signals.

## Core Capabilities

### News Sentiment Analysis
- **Article Classification**: Positive, negative, neutral sentiment classification
- **Entity Recognition**: Company, sector, and market mentions extraction
- **Impact Assessment**: News significance and market impact evaluation
- **Real-time Processing**: Live news feed analysis and sentiment scoring

### Social Media Analytics
- **Platform Coverage**: Twitter, Reddit, StockTwits, and financial forums
- **Influencer Analysis**: Key opinion leaders and their market influence
- **Trend Detection**: Viral sentiment shifts and social media trends
- **Volume Analysis**: Social media activity levels and engagement metrics

### Market Sentiment Indicators
- **Put/Call Ratio**: Options market sentiment and positioning
- **VIX Analysis**: Implied volatility as fear gauge
- **AAII Sentiment Survey**: Investor sentiment and positioning data
- **Commitment of Traders**: Institutional positioning and sentiment

## Data Sources

### News and Media Sources
- **Financial News APIs**: Bloomberg, Reuters, CNBC, and financial news feeds
- **Business News**: Wall Street Journal, Financial Times, and business publications
- **Regulatory Filings**: SEC filings, earnings reports, and corporate communications
- **Economic News**: Economic data releases and policy announcements

### Social Media Platforms
- **Twitter/X**: Real-time sentiment and trending topics
- **Reddit**: WallStreetBets, r/investing, and financial subreddits
- **StockTwits**: Stock-specific social sentiment and discussions
- **Financial Forums**: Seeking Alpha, Motley Fool, and investment communities

### Alternative Sentiment Data
- **Survey Data**: Investor confidence surveys and positioning data
- **Analyst Reports**: Buy/sell ratings and price target sentiment
- **Insider Trading**: Corporate insider activity and sentiment signals
- **Crowdfunding**: Kickstarter and Indiegogo sentiment for emerging companies

## LLM Integration

### Advanced Sentiment Processing
- **Contextual Analysis**: Understanding sentiment in market and economic context
- **Sarcasm Detection**: Identifying ironic or sarcastic social media posts
- **Impact Weighting**: Assessing the market influence of different sentiment sources
- **Behavioral Pattern Recognition**: Identifying recurring sentiment-driven market patterns

### Research Workflow
1. **Multi-Source Aggregation**: Collect sentiment data from diverse sources
2. **Quality Filtering**: Remove noise and low-quality sentiment signals
3. **LLM Deep Analysis**: Comprehensive sentiment interpretation and market implications
4. **Signal Generation**: Create actionable sentiment-based trading signals
5. **Collaborative Validation**: Cross-reference with other data subagents

## Collaborative Memory System

### Memory Architecture
- **Sentiment Pattern Storage**: Maintains historical sentiment patterns and market correlations
- **Source Reliability Tracking**: Tracks accuracy and predictive power of sentiment sources
- **Behavioral Insights**: Stores knowledge about sentiment-driven market behavior
- **Event Response Patterns**: Records market reactions to sentiment events

### Memory Management
- **Session-Based Storage**: Memory exists only during active research sessions
- **Collaborative Sharing**: Enables cross-subagent validation and enhancement
- **Base Agent Transfer**: Delivers sentiment analysis with complete context
- **Cleanup Protocol**: Automatic deletion of temporary sentiment data after transfer

## Sentiment Analysis Techniques

### Natural Language Processing
- **Text Classification**: Sentiment polarity and intensity scoring
- **Named Entity Recognition**: Identifying companies, people, and market references
- **Topic Modeling**: Extracting themes and topics from text data
- **Emotion Detection**: Identifying fear, greed, optimism, and pessimism

### Quantitative Sentiment Metrics
- **Sentiment Scores**: Normalized sentiment indices across different sources
- **Sentiment Volatility**: Measuring sentiment stability and extreme readings
- **Sentiment Momentum**: Rate of change in sentiment indicators
- **Sentiment Divergence**: Contrarian signals when sentiment extremes occur

### Market-Specific Sentiment
- **Sector Sentiment**: Industry-specific sentiment analysis
- **Asset Class Sentiment**: Equity, bond, commodity, and currency sentiment
- **Geographic Sentiment**: Regional and country-specific market sentiment
- **Time-Based Sentiment**: Intraday, daily, and longer-term sentiment trends

## Integration with Base DataAgent

### Communication Protocol
- **Sentiment Reports**: Structured sentiment analysis with confidence scores
- **Alert System**: Real-time sentiment alerts and threshold breaches
- **Market Impact Assessment**: Expected market reactions to sentiment shifts
- **Trading Signals**: Sentiment-based entry and exit signals

### Collaborative Enhancement
- **Market Data Integration**: Correlation with price action and volume patterns
- **Economic Data**: Economic context for sentiment interpretation
- **Options Data**: Impact on volatility and options positioning
- **Technical Analysis**: Sentiment confirmation for technical signals

## Risk Management

### Sentiment Risk Factors
- **False Signals**: Sentiment extremes that don't lead to market moves
- **Manipulation Risk**: Coordinated sentiment manipulation or pump-and-dump schemes
- **Noise vs Signal**: Distinguishing meaningful sentiment from market noise
- **Lag Effects**: Delayed market reactions to sentiment changes

### Validation Techniques
- **Backtesting**: Historical performance of sentiment signals
- **Cross-Validation**: Comparing sentiment signals across multiple sources
- **Market Correlation**: Relationship between sentiment and price movements
- **False Positive Analysis**: Frequency of sentiment signals that don't materialize

## Performance Tracking

### Sentiment Accuracy Metrics
- **Predictive Power**: Historical accuracy of sentiment signals
- **Market Timing**: Lead/lag relationship between sentiment and price action
- **Signal Strength**: Magnitude of market moves following sentiment signals
- **Source Reliability**: Performance comparison across different sentiment sources

### Analysis Quality
- **Processing Speed**: Real-time sentiment analysis capabilities
- **Coverage Breadth**: Number and diversity of sentiment sources
- **False Positive Rate**: Frequency of incorrect sentiment signals
- **Market Impact**: Average market reaction to sentiment alerts
