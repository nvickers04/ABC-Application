# News Data Subagent

## Overview
The News Data Subagent specializes in real-time news aggregation, sentiment analysis, and market impact assessment. It provides comprehensive news intelligence to understand market-moving events, sentiment shifts, and information flow dynamics.

## Core Capabilities

### Real-Time News Aggregation
- **Breaking News**: Instant capture of market-moving headlines and announcements
- **Source Diversity**: News from financial publications, wire services, and social media
- **Geographic Coverage**: Global news with regional market impact analysis
- **Event Classification**: Automated categorization of news by type and significance

### News Sentiment Analysis
- **Sentiment Scoring**: Multi-dimensional sentiment analysis of news content
- **Impact Assessment**: Historical analysis of news impact on price movements
- **Credibility Scoring**: News source reliability and bias assessment
- **Trend Analysis**: Sentiment trend identification and momentum analysis

### Market Event Detection
- **Major Announcements**: Earnings, M&A, economic data, and policy news
- **Crisis Detection**: Market crisis and black swan event identification
- **Regulatory News**: SEC filings, regulatory changes, and compliance news
- **Executive Changes**: Management changes and corporate governance news

### Information Flow Analysis
- **News Velocity**: Rate of news flow and information overload assessment
- **Market Reaction**: Historical price reactions to similar news events
- **Contagion Effects**: Cross-market impact of news events
- **Timing Analysis**: Optimal timing for news-based trading strategies

## Data Sources

### Primary News Feeds
- **Financial News Wires**: Bloomberg, Reuters, Dow Jones Newswires
- **Business Publications**: Wall Street Journal, Financial Times, Bloomberg
- **Regulatory Filings**: SEC EDGAR, corporate press releases
- **Economic Data**: Government economic releases and central bank communications

### Alternative Data Sources
- **Social Media**: Twitter, StockTwits, Reddit for retail sentiment
- **Industry Reports**: Analyst reports and industry-specific news
- **Corporate Communications**: Earnings calls, investor presentations
- **Government Sources**: Economic data releases and policy announcements

## LLM Integration

### News Intelligence Analysis
- **Context Understanding**: Deep analysis of news significance and implications
- **Market Impact Prediction**: Assessment of potential price movement magnitude
- **Sentiment Interpretation**: Multi-layered sentiment analysis beyond simple positive/negative
- **Event Chain Analysis**: Understanding interconnected news events and their combined impact

### Research Workflow
1. **News Aggregation**: Collect and categorize incoming news data
2. **Impact Assessment**: Evaluate potential market impact and significance
3. **LLM Analysis**: Deep evaluation of news content and market implications
4. **Signal Generation**: Create actionable signals from news intelligence
5. **Collaborative Sharing**: Distribute news insights to other subagents

## Collaborative Memory System

### Memory Architecture
- **News Pattern Storage**: Maintains news impact patterns and market reactions
- **Sentiment History**: Tracks sentiment trends and their market correlations
- **Event Response Memory**: Records market reactions to various news events
- **Source Credibility**: Maintains news source reliability assessments

### Memory Management
- **Session-Based Storage**: Memory exists during active research sessions
- **Collaborative Sharing**: Enables cross-subagent validation and enhancement
- **Base Agent Transfer**: Delivers news intelligence with complete context
- **Cleanup Protocol**: Automatic deletion of temporary news analysis data

## Data Types and Formats

### News Content Data
- **Headlines and Summaries**: Key news content and executive summaries
- **Full Articles**: Complete news articles with detailed analysis
- **Metadata**: Publication time, source, author, and credibility scores
- **Categorization**: News type, affected assets, geographic scope

### Sentiment Data
- **Sentiment Scores**: Multi-dimensional sentiment metrics
- **Confidence Levels**: Sentiment analysis confidence and reliability
- **Trend Indicators**: Sentiment momentum and trend strength
- **Comparative Analysis**: Sentiment vs historical averages and peers

### Impact Data
- **Price Reactions**: Historical price movements following news events
- **Volume Changes**: Trading volume changes associated with news
- **Volatility Impact**: Changes in volatility following news events
- **Market Breadth**: Breadth of market impact across sectors and assets

- **Sentiment Updates**: Continuous sentiment analysis and trend updates
- **Event Notifications**: Major market event notifications with context

### Collaborative Enhancement
- **Market Data Integration**: News impact correlation with price action
- **Sentiment Analysis**: News sentiment integration with broader sentiment data
- **Economic Data**: News context for economic data interpretation
- **Options Data**: News-driven volatility and options pricing analysis
