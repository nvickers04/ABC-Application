# Institutional Data Subagent

## Overview
The Institutional Data Subagent specializes in institutional holdings analysis, ETF flows, and smart money positioning. It provides deep insights into institutional investor behavior, large block trades, and market impact from professional money management.

## Core Capabilities

### Institutional Holdings Analysis
- **13F Filings**: Quarterly institutional holdings data from SEC filings
- **Position Changes**: Tracking institutional position adjustments and portfolio rebalancing
- **Top Holdings**: Analysis of most concentrated institutional positions
- **Sector Allocation**: Institutional sector preferences and allocation trends

### ETF Flow Analysis
- **Institutional ETF Flows**: Daily/weekly ETF inflow and outflow data
- **Smart Money Tracking**: Institutional money movement patterns
- **Flow Attribution**: Sector and asset class flow analysis
- **Impact Assessment**: Market impact of institutional ETF flows

### Dark Pool and Block Trade Detection
- **Large Block Trades**: Identification of significant institutional trades
- **Dark Pool Activity**: Off-exchange trading pattern analysis
- **Trade Size Analysis**: Distribution of trade sizes and market impact
- **Anonymity Analysis**: Dark pool vs lit market trading preferences

### Institutional Sentiment Indicators
- **Smart Money Positioning**: Institutional long/short positioning analysis
- **Crowded Trades**: Identification of consensus institutional positions
- **Risk Positioning**: Institutional risk exposure and hedging patterns
- **Market Timing**: Institutional entry/exit timing analysis

## Data Sources

### Primary Institutional Data
- **SEC EDGAR**: 13F filings and institutional disclosure data
- **ETF Flow Providers**: Real-time ETF flow data and analytics
- **Broker Reports**: Institutional positioning and activity reports
- **Regulatory Filings**: Additional institutional disclosure requirements

### Alternative Data Sources
- **Dark Pool Aggregators**: Consolidated dark pool trading data
- **Institutional Research**: Broker research and institutional surveys
- **Market Intelligence**: Third-party institutional activity reports
- **Proprietary Data**: Direct relationships with institutional data providers

## LLM Integration

### Institutional Behavior Analysis
