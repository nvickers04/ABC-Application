# Kalshi Data Subagent

## Overview
The Kalshi Data Subagent specializes in event contract data and prediction market intelligence. It provides insights into crowd-sourced probability assessments, event-driven trading opportunities, and market expectations for future events.

## Core Capabilities

### Event Contract Analysis
- **Contract Universe**: Comprehensive analysis of available event contracts
- **Probability Tracking**: Real-time probability changes for various events
- **Contract Design**: Understanding contract structure and payout mechanisms
- **Market Efficiency**: Assessment of prediction market efficiency and accuracy

### Prediction Market Intelligence
- **Crowd Wisdom**: Aggregation of market participant expectations
- **Event Probability**: Market-implied probabilities for future events
- **Risk Premia**: Event risk pricing and market sentiment assessment
- **Market Timing**: Optimal timing for event-driven trading strategies

### Event Impact Analysis
- **Historical Accuracy**: Track record of prediction market accuracy
- **Market Reactions**: Price movements following event outcomes
- **Contagion Effects**: Cross-market impact of event resolutions
- **Expectation Management**: Market expectation vs actual outcome analysis

### Risk Assessment
- **Event Risk Pricing**: Market pricing of event uncertainty
- **Tail Risk Analysis**: Extreme event probability assessment
- **Correlation Analysis**: Event correlation with traditional markets
- **Hedging Opportunities**: Event-driven hedging strategies

## Data Sources

### Primary Prediction Market Data
- **Kalshi API**: Direct access to event contract data and probabilities
- **Contract Specifications**: Detailed contract terms and conditions
- **Trade Data**: Real-time and historical trading data
- **Market Statistics**: Volume, open interest, and market metrics

### Alternative Data Sources
- **Other Prediction Markets**: Polymarket, PredictIt, and other platforms
- **News and Events**: Real-world event data for validation
- **Economic Indicators**: Macro data correlation with event probabilities
- **Social Sentiment**: Social media sentiment around predicted events

## LLM Integration

### Prediction Market Analysis
- **Event Understanding**: Deep analysis of event significance and implications
- **Probability Assessment**: Evaluation of market-implied probabilities
- **Market Efficiency**: Assessment of prediction market rationality
- **Trading Opportunities**: Identification of mispriced event contracts

### Research Workflow
1. **Contract Analysis**: Review available event contracts and market data
2. **Probability Evaluation**: Assess market probabilities and crowd wisdom
3. **LLM Analysis**: Deep evaluation of event implications and market efficiency
4. **Signal Generation**: Create event-driven trading signals and recommendations
5. **Collaborative Sharing**: Distribute prediction market insights to other subagents

## Collaborative Memory System

### Memory Architecture
- **Event Pattern Storage**: Maintains event outcome patterns and market reactions
- **Probability Memory**: Tracks prediction market accuracy and bias patterns
- **Contract Memory**: Records contract performance and market efficiency
- **Impact Analysis**: Stores market reaction patterns to event resolutions

### Memory Management
- **Session-Based Storage**: Memory exists during active research sessions
- **Collaborative Sharing**: Enables cross-subagent validation and enhancement
- **Base Agent Transfer**: Delivers prediction market intelligence with complete context
- **Cleanup Protocol**: Automatic deletion of temporary prediction data
